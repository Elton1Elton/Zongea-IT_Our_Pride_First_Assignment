Our Pride

National Anthem

All hail, Liberia, Hail! (All hail!)
This Glorious land of liberty
shall long be ours.

Though new her name,
Green be her fame,[a]
And mighty be her powers,
And mighty be her power,

In joy and gladness
With our hearts united,
We'll shout the freedom
Of a race benighted,

Long live Liberia, happy land!
A home of glorious liberty,
By God's command!

All hail, Liberia, hail! (All hail!)
In union strong success in sure
We cannot fail!

With God above Our rights to prove
We will o'er all prevail,
We will o'er all prevail,

With heart and hand
Our Country's cause defending
We'll meet he foe
With vlor unpretending.

Long live Liberia, happy land!
A home of glorious liberity,
By God's command!
